Vista Health Summaries

A Health Summary is a clinically oriented, structured report that extracts many kinds of data from VISTA web and displays it in a standard format. The individual patient is the focus of health summaries. The data displayed covers a wide range of health-related information such as demographic data, allergies, current active medical problems, laboratory results, etc.
